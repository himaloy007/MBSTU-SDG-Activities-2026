Cyber Security Club

The MBSTU Cyber Security Club has been actively promoting cyber awareness, digital literacy, and online safety at Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University (MBSTU).
Recently, the club successfully organized the program “Road to Cyber Security with Ostad”, in collaboration with Ostad, MBSTU Photographic Society, and MBSTU Career Club.
This initiative empowered students with essential cybersecurity knowledge and hands-on insights, fostering a culture of responsible digital engagement and technological excellence.

In addition, as part of the Cyber Security Awareness Month 2025, the club conducted a series of awareness campaigns and training activities throughout October to enhance the university community’s understanding of cyber threats and data protection measures.

Alignment with UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

These initiatives contribute directly to the following SDGs:

SDG 4 – Quality Education: Promoting digital literacy and awareness among students.

SDG 9 – Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure: Encouraging innovation through responsible use of technology and secure digital practices.

SDG 17 – Partnerships for the Goals: Strengthening collaboration between academia and industry partners like Ostad.
